Xia-Hai City God （霞海城隍, Xia-Hai Chenghuang）

Located on Dihua Street of Taipei, Xia-Hai City God Temple is the most famous City God Temple in Taipei. In 19th century, immigrants from Quanzhou, Fujian brought the statue of Xia-Hai City God to Taipei and he soon became the city god of Taipei.

Xia-Hai City God Temple only takes the area of around 200 square meters, but there are more than 600 statues enshrined here, making it the "most crowded" temple in Taiwan.
